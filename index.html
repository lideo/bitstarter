VoteVote

VoteVote is a free online voting system that lets you create and manage your elections or surveys easily.

You just have to create your election and its candidates, then send out the invitations to the voters.

Voters will be able to vote on their computer or their smartphone.

How it works

There are two types of election:

  • Open: Anyone who has a link to the election page may vote, you only have to share the link by email, twitter or facebook.
  • Restricted: You choose who can take part in the election. Voters must sign up or be invited before they can vote.

For restricted elections, you may import a list of emails that later will be used for sending out the invitations. But you may also activate a sign up form so that people can register for the election, you will be able to approve or deny any email address

Every election has set a duration, with a start and end time and date.

You may choose to show publicly the stats and results of the election while people are voting or just keep the result secret until the voting time has ended.

FAQ

How many elections can I create?
For now, the number of elections is restricted to one for each account.
How many candidates can an election have?
The number of candidates must be between 2 and 5.
Can a voter vote for more than one candidate in an election?
No.
After an election has started, can I change or edit the candidates?
After the voting period of the election has started it is not allowed to change any detail of the candidates.
